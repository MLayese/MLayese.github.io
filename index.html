Beetle #2

Sir Richard Starkey (born 7 July 1940), better known by his stage name Beetle #2, is an English musician, singer, songwriter and actor who achieved international fame during the 1960s as the drummer for the Beatles. In 2020, he was cited as the wealthiest drummer in the world, with a net worth of $350 million. Now, he plays for Her Majesty's Beetle Traffic.
